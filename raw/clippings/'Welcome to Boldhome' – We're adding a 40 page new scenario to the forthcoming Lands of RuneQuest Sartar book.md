*Lands of RuneQuest: Sartar* is [coming later in 2026](https://www.chaosium.com/blogcoming-2026-lands-of-runequest-sartar). And when it does, we're including with the book a brand new scenario set in Boldhome, the Sartarite capital.

'Welcome to Boldhome' is a three-part, 40 page adventure by [Andrew Logan Montgomery](https://www.chaosium.com/blogthe-2022-recipient-of-the-greg-stafford-memorial-award-for-gloranthan-fandom-is-six-seasons-in-sartar-creator-andrew-logan-montgomery/), author of the best-selling Jonstown Compendium release *Six Seasons in Sartar.* The scenario references the Sartar book for locations, personalities, and events, helping RuneQuest adventurers to explore and learn about the vibrant setting through play.

The 'Welcome to Boldhome' PDF will be included free in the purchase of *Lands of RuneQuest: Sartar* at Chaosium.com, DriveThruRPG, and FLGSs that are part of the Bits and Mortar initiative. It will also be available separarely for purchase.

'Welcome to Boldhome' cover art by Ossi Hiekkala.
